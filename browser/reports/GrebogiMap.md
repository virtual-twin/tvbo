

# GrebogiMap
GrebogiMap

## Equations


### State Equations
$$
\theta = \left(\theta + a*\sin{\left(2*\theta \right)} - b*\sin{\left(4*\theta \right)} - x*\sin{\left(\theta \right)}\right) \bmod 2*\pi
$$
$$
x = - J_{0}*\cos{\left(\theta \right)}
$$


## Parameters

| **Parameter** | **Value** | **Unit** | **Description** |
|---------------|-----------|----------|-----------------|
| $a$ | 1.32 | N/A | Coefficient a in angle update. |
| $b$ | 0.9 | N/A | Coefficient b in angle update. |
| $J_{0}$ | 0.3 | N/A | Drive amplitude J0. |
