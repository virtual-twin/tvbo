

# ForcedPendulum
ForcedPendulum

## Equations


### State Equations
$$
\frac{d}{d t} x = v
$$
$$
\frac{d}{d t} v = - \sin{\left(x \right)} + f*\cos{\left(\omega*t \right)} - d*v
$$


## Parameters

| **Parameter** | **Value** | **Unit** | **Description** |
|---------------|-----------|----------|-----------------|
| $\omega$ | 2.2 | N/A | Driving angular frequency Ï‰. |
| $f$ | 27.0 | N/A | Driving amplitude f. |
| $d$ | 0.2 | N/A | Linear damping coefficient d. |
