

# Halvorsen
Halvorsen

## Equations


### State Equations
$$
\frac{d}{d t} x = - y^{2} - 4*y - 4*z - a*x
$$
$$
\frac{d}{d t} y = - z^{2} - 4*x - 4*z - a*y
$$
$$
\frac{d}{d t} z = c - x^{2} - 4*x - 4*y - a*z
$$


## Parameters

| **Parameter** | **Value** | **Unit** | **Description** |
|---------------|-----------|----------|-----------------|
| $a$ | 1.4 | N/A | Linear damping / coupling strength. |
| $b$ | 0.4 | N/A | Quadratic saturation coefficient. |
| $c$ | 1.0 | N/A | Constant drive parameter. |
