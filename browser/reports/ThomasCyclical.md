

# ThomasCyclical
ThomasCyclical

## Equations


### State Equations
$$
\frac{d}{d t} x = - b*x + \sin{\left(y \right)}
$$
$$
\frac{d}{d t} y = - b*y + \sin{\left(z \right)}
$$
$$
\frac{d}{d t} z = - b*z + \sin{\left(x \right)}
$$


## Parameters

| **Parameter** | **Value** | **Unit** | **Description** |
|---------------|-----------|----------|-----------------|
| $b$ | 0.2 | N/A | Linear damping parameter b. |
