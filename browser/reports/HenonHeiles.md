## Equations

### Derived Variables
$$
H = - \frac{y^{3}}{3} + 0.5*px^{2} + 0.5*py^{2} + 0.5*x^{2} + 0.5*y^{2} + y*x^{2}
$$

### State Equations
$$
\frac{d}{d t} x = px
$$
$$
\frac{d}{d t} y = py
$$
$$
\frac{d}{d t} px = - x - 2*x*y
$$
$$
\frac{d}{d t} py = y^{2} - y - x^{2}
$$
