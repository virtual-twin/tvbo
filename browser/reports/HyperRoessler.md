## Equations


### State Equations
$$
\frac{d}{d t} x = - y - z
$$
$$
\frac{d}{d t} y = w + x + a*y
$$
$$
\frac{d}{d t} z = b + z*\left(x - c\right)
$$
$$
\frac{d}{d t} w = w*y - d*z
$$
