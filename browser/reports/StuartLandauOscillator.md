

# StuartLandauOscillator
StuartLandauOscillator

## Equations


### State Equations
$$
\frac{d}{d t} x = x*\left(\mu - x^{2} - y^{2}\right) - \omega*y - b*y*\left(x^{2} + y^{2}\right)
$$
$$
\frac{d}{d t} y = \omega*x + y*\left(\mu - x^{2} - y^{2}\right) + b*x*\left(x^{2} + y^{2}\right)
$$


## Parameters

| **Parameter** | **Value** | **Unit** | **Description** |
|---------------|-----------|----------|-----------------|
| $\mu$ | 1.0 | N/A | Linear growth (bifurcation) parameter μ. |
| $\omega$ | 1.0 | N/A | Base angular frequency ω. |
| $b$ | 1.0 | N/A | Nonlinear frequency shift coefficient b. |
