

# StommelThermohaline
StommelThermohaline

## Equations


### State Equations
$$
\frac{d}{d t} T = \eta_{1} - T - T*\left|{S - T}\right|
$$
$$
\frac{d}{d t} S = \eta_{2} - S*\eta_{3} - S*\left|{S - T}\right|
$$


## Parameters

| **Parameter** | **Value** | **Unit** | **Description** |
|---------------|-----------|----------|-----------------|
| $\eta_{1}$ | 3.0 | N/A | Thermal forcing parameter η₁. |
| $\eta_{2}$ | 1.0 | N/A | Freshwater/salinity source parameter η₂. |
| $\eta_{3}$ | 0.3 | N/A | Salinity damping coefficient η₃. |
