

# Rikitake
Rikitake

## Equations


### State Equations
$$
\frac{d}{d t} x = y*z - \mu*x
$$
$$
\frac{d}{d t} y = x*\left(z - \alpha\right) - \mu*y
$$
$$
\frac{d}{d t} z = 1 - x*y
$$


## Parameters

| **Parameter** | **Value** | **Unit** | **Description** |
|---------------|-----------|----------|-----------------|
| $\mu$ | 1.0 | N/A | Dissipation parameter μ. |
| $\alpha$ | 1.0 | N/A | Axial asymmetry parameter α. |
