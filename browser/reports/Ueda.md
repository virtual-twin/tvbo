Ueda

## Equations


### State Equations
$$
\frac{d}{d t} x = v
$$
$$
\frac{d}{d t} v = - x^{3} + B*\cos{\left(t \right)} - k*v
$$


## Parameters

| **Parameter** | **Value** | **Unit** | **Description** |
|---------------|-----------|----------|-----------------|
| $k$ | 0.1 | N/A | Linear damping coefficient k. |
| $B$ | 12.0 | N/A | Forcing amplitude B. |
