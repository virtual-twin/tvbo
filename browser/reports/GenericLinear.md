

# GenericLinear
The Generic Linear (GL) is a phenomenological, linear one-dimensional  (i.e., one state-variable ('x')) neural mass model (Gal√°n, 2008).

## Equations


### State Equations
$$
\frac{d}{d t} x = c_{global} + \gamma*x + c_{local}*x
$$


## Parameters

| **Parameter** | **Value** | **Unit** | **Description** |
|---------------|-----------|----------|-----------------|
| $\gamma$ | -10.0 | N/A | Slope parameter of the state-variable 'x_GL', representing the inverse of the relaxation time |
