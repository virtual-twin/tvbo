

# Lorenz84
Lorenz84

## Equations


### State Equations
$$
\frac{d}{d t} x = - y^{2} - z^{2} + F*a - a*x
$$
$$
\frac{d}{d t} y = G - y + x*y - b*x*z
$$
$$
\frac{d}{d t} z = - z + x*z + b*x*y
$$
